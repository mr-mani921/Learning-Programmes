Definitions

Fruit
A fruit is the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seeds and is edible in its raw state.
Vegetable
A vegetable is a part of a plant, such as the roots, stems, leaves, or flowers, that is typically savory or less sweet and is often used in cooking.
