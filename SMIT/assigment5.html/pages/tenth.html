The Alchemist

By: Paulo Coelho

Summary

"The Alchemist" is a philosophical novel that follows Santiago, a young shepherd, on his journey to find treasure at the Egyptian pyramids. Along the way, he meets mentors and faces challenges that teach him about the importance of listening to one's heart, recognizing opportunities, and pursuing dreams.
