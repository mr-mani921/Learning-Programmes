Introduction to Computer Science

A foundational course for understanding the principles of computing.

Course Description:

This course introduces the basics of computer science, including algorithms, data structures, and programming concepts. Students will gain hands-on experience with coding and problem-solving techniques.

Syllabus:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Computing and History of Computers
  • Week 2: Basics of Programming and Algorithms
  • Week 3: Data Structures and Their Applications
  • Week 4: Object-Oriented Programming Concepts
  • Week 5: Basics of Databases and SQL
  • Week 6: Introduction to Web Development
  • Week 7: Final Project and Presentations
